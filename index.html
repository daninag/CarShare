About

Carshare is a European carpooling digital startup (website & app) that caters for drivers and passengers from the learner to the aficionado. Drivers and passengers Offer A Ride / Find A Ride via the website and / or app. Following a successful match the designated parties contact each other re: travel arrangements (i.e. cost, meeting place, etc). Then they meet up and take off together as planned. 


In addition, Carshare is a national online sustainable transport information hub. Carshare capitalizes on the ecological and societal benefits of carsharing. Thus, low-cost, communal and environmentally friendly travel is only a few clicks away. Carshare encourages its users to consider and practice novel approaches to car dependency. Carshare ultimately hopes to ease the economical, ecological and societal costs of modern motoring. This start-up verges towards creating an optimal digital and real-world experience for its users. Carshare is essentially organized, pre-arranged informal carsharing between strangers / members who enjoy independence, exiting experiences and flexibility. Notwithstanding, Carshare enables strangers / members to set up impromptu ad hoc rides, via the website and app.
